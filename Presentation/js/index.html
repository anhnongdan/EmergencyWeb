The Motivation

  • Difficulties of searching and rescuing victims on devastated areas.
  • "Near" means "fast". Local support is crucial
  • Need a reliable and easy deployable community network.
  • The advantage of Mesh Wireless Network on establishing and expanding connection coverage.

The web page

  • Two queues of victims
    • Recommending list: Victims who have sent messages will be add into this queue
    • Processing list: Queue of victims who are going to be rescued with order determined by The Rescue Commander
  • Victims can be switched between two queues
  • The two queue will be effective with multi-interface system
